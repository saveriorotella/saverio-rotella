I am a second-year Research Master's student in International Relations at the [University of Groningen](https://www.rug.nl/). I am specialising in quantitative methods to study democratisation processes, democratic erosion, and populism.

Before joining the University of Groningen, I received a BA in Political Science and International Relation at the [University of Perugia](https://www.unipg.it/en/) with a 110/110 cum laude. I also conducted two EU-funded study exchanges (Erasmus+) at [KU Leuven](https://www.kuleuven.be/english/kuleuven) and the [University of Antwerp](https://www.uantwerpen.be/en/), and a EU-funded traineeship at the University of Groningen.

My research and commentary have appeared in various Italian and European think tanks and outlets — you can find my work [here](https://saveriorotella.github.io/year-archive/).
